Manali

Bhrigu Lake in Manali is situated at about 14000 feet above the sea level. It is said that the lake never freezes completely during winters. Manali is also a pioneer of many aero sports. In all Manali is wholesome of refreshments and excitements. Manali is considered as God’s heaven.From majestic mountains to long rivers to aromatic forest and an outstanding blend of the colours of nature, Manali has its all. Manali is believed to be created after the name of Manu rishi. It is believed that Manu rishi tends to meditate at this place. Trekking at Manali is considered most arduous on the other hand most exciting by a majority of trekkers.

Leh ladakh

The Ladakh capital city of Leh lies near the eastern parts of Jammu and Kashmir, on the crossroads of the historic 'Silk Route' from Sinkiang to West Asia and to the plains of India.Leh was for centuries an important stopover on trade routes along the Indus Valley between Tibet, Kashmir, India and China. The main goods carried were salt, grain, pashm or cashmere wool, charas or cannabis resin from the Tarim Basin, indigo, silk yarn and Banaras brocade.
